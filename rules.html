Basic Eligibility

1. You must be between 18 and 65 years of age to donate blood.
2. You must weigh at least 50 kg (110 lbs).
3. You should be in good general health and feeling well on the day of donation.
4. It is important to be hydrated before and after donating blood.

Health Conditions

5. Individuals with a history of heart disease, hypertension, or diabetes should consult a physician before donating.
6. Those who have undergone surgery should wait at least 6 months before donating blood.
7. Do not donate blood if you have a cold, flu, sore throat, or any other infection.
8. If you have recently received a vaccination, please wait at least 2 weeks before donating blood.
9. If you have a history of hepatitis or malaria, consult the blood bank before donating.

Travel History

10. If you have traveled to areas where certain infectious diseases are prevalent, consult the blood bank before donation.
11. Individuals who have lived abroad for more than 5 years should check for eligibility.

Blood Donation Frequency

12. You can donate whole blood every 56 days (8 weeks) or plasma every 28 days.
13. Female donors should wait for at least 4 months between blood donations.
14. Male donors can donate blood every 3 months.

Additional Rules

15. Pregnant women are not eligible to donate blood and should wait for 6 months after delivery.
16. Ensure you have eaten a healthy meal within 3 hours before donating blood.
17. Avoid alcohol for at least 24 hours before donating blood.
18. Avoid strenuous exercise or heavy lifting for at least 24 hours after donation.
19. Keep your arm bandaged for at least 4-5 hours post-donation.
20. Inform the blood bank staff if you are taking any medication.

After Blood Donation

21. Relax and stay hydrated for at least 15 minutes at the blood bank after your donation.
22. Eat a healthy snack and drink fluids immediately after donating blood.
23. If you experience dizziness or lightheadedness, sit down or lie down until you feel better.
24. Contact the blood bank immediately if you feel unwell in the days following your donation.
